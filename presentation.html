Obligation to Pay:

Law states:

Both BHP and USO are two components of non-tax fees that must be paid by every business actor who has obtained a license to operate a telecommunications network.

Calculation of BHP & USO:

BHP: 0.50% Directorate GeneralCompensation for the permits they obtain in the implementation of telecommunications networks and/or services
USO: 1.25% BAKTIObligation that must be paid through BAKTI by BUP in the form of: Provision of telecommunications networks and/or services

Dispute

How the regulation of BUP obligations regarding BHP and USO payments is related to the type of BUP business activities and PPP Agreements.

Confusion between payments for Capital Expenditure & Operating Expenditure

Advice

Recommendation to separate the recording of AP components paid to BUP into AP for capital expenditure (capex) and AP for operating expenditure (opex) both in the current Palapa Ring Project and for future telecommunications projects

Implementation

  • AP receipts for the capital expenditure (capex) portion are 'Construction and infrastructure development services' receipts and therefore are not taken into account as gross income in calculating BHP and USO payments.

  • In this case, what is included in the calculation of gross income in the calculation is the 'Service Expense'. Based on the AP payment system which refers to the Financial Accounting Standard (ISAK 16), so that the recording of AP receipts by BUP needs to be separated between AP receipts for the Capital Expenditure (capex) portion and AP for Operating Expenditure (opex).
